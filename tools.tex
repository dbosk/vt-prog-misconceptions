\subsection{Tools}

The choice of development tool shapes which errors students encounter and how
they recover from them. Whether a particular IDE is best for CS1, and whether
one should be recommended, is beyond our scope; the literature does note,
however, that tooling can surface the syntactic errors that novices make most
often:
\blockcquote{MisconceptionsSurvey2017}{Although many other syntactic-level errors
are reported in previous research (see Altadmri and Brown (2015), Hristova et
al.\ (2003), and Sorva (2012)), we do not discuss them in depth here, because
problems in syntactic knowledge are often easy to detect and fix. Perhaps that
is why they are often noted as the most frequent mistakes novices make. A
compiler or a modern integrated development environment (IDE) may be able to
find them and then provide error messages or hints for correction.}
